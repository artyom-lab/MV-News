Lanning's Foods is now being operated by the highly capable hands of third -generation family members: Zach Phillippi, Austen Gilardi and Steven Gilardi.

All three of these gentlemen are lifelong residents of Knox County. They are active members of the community and fully intend to raise their families in the Knox county area. They are very excited to experience the future changes of the industry and adapt the business practices to accommodate the ever-changing needs of the fresh food industry.

The mission now and always has been to offer the freshest and healthiest foods available at an affordable price to our community and surrounding area today and for many years to come.
